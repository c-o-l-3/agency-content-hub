## Competitive Positioning

**Primary Competitive Set:**
1. Beach Wedding Services — direct beach ceremonies
2. Resort Wedding Packages — Grand Wailea, Four Seasons
3. Other Maui Chapels — Maria Lanakila Church
4. Kauai/Big Island Venues — competing Hawaiian islands

**Key Differentiators:**
- Weather-proof (covered chapel)
- Intimate (not resort ballroom)
- Tropical branding (vs traditional church)
- Maui's wedding industry maturity

**Defensible Moat:** "Intimate Hawaiian chapel experience with weather reliability"
